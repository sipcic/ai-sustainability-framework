## How to Cite the Dataset and Frameworks
When discussing Section 4 measurement design, cite both **empirical data repositories** and **methodological precedents**:

- **World Bank, OECD, ILO, IEA, and UNDP datasets** for raw measurement data.  
- **Stiglitz-Sen-Fitoussi (2009)** and **Raworth (2017)** for the theoretical foundation of “beyond-GDP” measures.  
- **Ekins & Zenghelis (2022)** for green-growth metrics linked to equilibrium models.  
- **Sachs et al. (2023)** for the SDG integration framework.
